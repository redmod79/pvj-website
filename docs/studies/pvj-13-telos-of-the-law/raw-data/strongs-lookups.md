# Raw Strong's Lookups

## G5056 (telos) -- Lexicon
Transliteration: telos
Pronunciation: tel
POS: neuter noun
Definition: from primary tello (to set out for a definite point or goal); properly the point aimed at as a limit; the conclusion of an act or state; result; purpose; toll/tax
BLB count: 42
Translations: end (14x), the end (13x), custom (3x), shall the end (2x), an end (2x), Finally (1x), the uttermost (1x), ends (1x), the ending (1x), her continual (1x)

## G4137 (pleroo) -- Lexicon
Transliteration: pleroo
Pronunciation: play-ro
POS: verb
Definition: to make replete, i.e. (literally) to cram (a net), level up
BLB count: 90
Translations: fulfilled and variants (~50x), filled (4x), complete (2x), full (2x), various other

## G2647 (kataluo) -- used in Mat 5:17
Transliteration: kataluo
POS: verb
Definition: to demolish, to destroy, throw down; to dissolve
Used by Jesus: "I am not come to kataluo the law"
Used by Paul: "if I build again the things which I kataluo'd" (Gal 2:18)
