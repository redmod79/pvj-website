### pvj-04 Key Finding (Dependency)
Paul's semantic range for pistis, ergon, dikaiosyne, nomos, and charis is a proper superset of Jesus's. Paul uses all senses Jesus uses plus additional forensic/soteriological senses. The widest vocabulary gap is charis (grace).
