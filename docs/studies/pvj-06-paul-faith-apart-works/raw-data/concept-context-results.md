# Concept Context Results (pvj-06)

## ROM 3:28 --scope author
Key Paul parallels:
- Gal 2:16 (exact formula: not justified by works of law, but by faith of Christ)
- Gal 3:11 (no man justified by law, the just shall live by faith)
- Gal 3:24 (law as schoolmaster unto Christ, justified by faith)
- Rom 10:4 (Christ end of law for righteousness to every believer)
- Rom 4:13 (promise through righteousness of faith, not through law)
- 1 Tim 1:9 (law not made for righteous man)

## ROM 2:13 --scope author
Key Paul parallels:
- Rom 10:4-5 (righteousness of law vs. righteousness of faith)

- Gal 2:16,21; 3:11,21,24; 5:4

## ROM 3:31 --scope author
