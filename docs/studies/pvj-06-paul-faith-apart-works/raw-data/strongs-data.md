# Strong's Data (pvj-06)

## G1344 (dikaioo) — justify
- Part of Speech: verb
- BLB Count: 40
- Definition: to render (show or regard as) just or innocent; to free, justify
- Key distribution: Romans 15 uses, Galatians 7 uses, James 2 uses
- Paul uses dikaioo 27 times (67.5% of NT occurrences)

## G4102 (pistis) — faith
- Part of Speech: feminine noun
- BLB Count: 244
- Definition: persuasion, credence; moral conviction of religious truth
- Heavy cluster in Romans 3-4 (15+ occurrences)

## G2041 (ergon) — work
- Part of Speech: neuter noun
- BLB Count: 176
- Definition: toil; an act, deed, doing, labor, work
- Paul negative (justification): Rom 3:20,27,28; 4:2,6; 9:12,32; 11:6; Gal 2:16; 3:2,5,10; Eph 2:9
- Paul positive (ethical): Rom 2:6,7,15; 13:3,12; Eph 2:10; 4:12; Col 1:10; 1 Tim 5:10; 6:18

## G3551 (nomos) — law
- Part of Speech: masculine noun
- BLB Count: 197
- Definition: anything established, a law; specifically the Mosaic law
- Romans has ~75 uses (highest density in NT)
- Semantic range: Mosaic Torah, principle/rule, OT Scriptures, moral standard
