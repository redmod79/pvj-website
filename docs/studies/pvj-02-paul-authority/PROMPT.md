# pvj-02: Paul's Claimed Relationship to Jesus and the Apostles

## Study Question

What is Paul's claimed relationship to Jesus and the other apostles? Investigate Galatians 1:1 (apostle not from men nor by man), Galatians 1:11-17 (gospel by revelation, did not confer with flesh and blood), Galatians 2:1-10 (Jerusalem council -- "they added nothing to me"), Galatians 2:6-9 (James, Cephas, John gave right hand of fellowship), 2 Peter 3:15-16 (Peter endorses Paul's writings as Scripture), Acts 9/22/26 (three conversion accounts). Does Paul claim parallel, subordinate, or independent authority to the Twelve? What does "my gospel" (Romans 2:16, 16:25, 2 Timothy 2:8) mean -- is it a different gospel or the same gospel from a different angle? Does Galatians 1:8 ("though we or an angel from heaven preach any other gospel, let him be accursed") indicate Paul's gospel could differ from the original?

## Key Verse Clusters

### Cluster 1: Paul's Apostolic Self-Claims
- Gal 1:1 -- apostle not of men nor by man but by Jesus Christ
- Rom 1:1 -- servant of Jesus Christ, called apostle, separated unto gospel
- 1 Cor 1:1 -- called apostle of Jesus Christ through the will of God
- 1 Cor 9:1 -- Am I not an apostle? Have I not seen Jesus Christ our Lord?
- 1 Cor 15:8-10 -- last of all, born out of due time, least of apostles
- 2 Cor 11:5 -- not behind the very chiefest apostles
- 2 Cor 12:11-12 -- signs of an apostle wrought among you

### Cluster 2: Paul's Gospel Source Claims
- Gal 1:11-12 -- gospel not after man, received by revelation of Jesus Christ
- Gal 1:15-17 -- called by grace, did not confer with flesh and blood
- 2 Cor 12:1-4 -- caught up to third heaven, heard unspeakable words
- Gal 1:8 -- though we or angel preach another gospel, let him be accursed

### Cluster 3: "My Gospel"
- Rom 2:16 -- according to my gospel
- Rom 16:25 -- according to my gospel and the preaching of Jesus Christ
- 2 Tim 2:8 -- raised from the dead according to my gospel

### Cluster 4: Relationship with Jerusalem Apostles
- Gal 1:18-19 -- went to see Peter after 3 years, 15 days, saw James
- Gal 2:1-5 -- went up by revelation, communicated gospel privately
- Gal 2:6-9 -- they added nothing, gave right hand of fellowship
- Gal 2:11-14 -- withstood Peter to the face at Antioch

### Cluster 5: External Validation
- 2 Pet 3:15-16 -- Peter endorses Paul's writings as Scripture
- Acts 9:15 -- chosen vessel before Gentiles, kings, Israel
- Acts 22:14-15 -- chosen to know God's will, see the Just One
- Acts 26:16-18 -- made minister and witness, sent to Gentiles

### Cluster 6: Conversion Accounts
- Acts 9:1-20, Acts 22:6-16, Acts 26:12-18

### Cluster 7: Unity of Gospel
- 1 Cor 15:11 -- whether I or they, so we preach, so ye believed
- Gal 2:7 -- gospel of uncircumcision committed to me, circumcision to Peter
