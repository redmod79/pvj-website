# pvj-02 Topics: Paul's Claimed Relationship to Jesus and the Apostles

## Primary Topics (from Naves Semantic Search)

### 1. APOSTLES (score: 0.56)
The Twelve were selected by Jesus (Luk 6:13), commissioned (Mat 10; 28:19-20; Mrk 3:14-15; Acts 1-2), given authority (Mat 16:19; 18:18; 19:28), given miraculous power (2 Cor 12:12), and inspired (Mat 10:27; Luk 24:45; Acts 1:2). False apostles are noted (2 Cor 11:13; Rev 2:2).

### 2. APOSTLE (score: 0.67)
An appellation of Jesus (Heb 3:1). See APOSTLES.

### 3. PAUL (score: 0.51)
Called to be an apostle (Acts 22:14-21; 26:16-18; Rom 1:1; 1 Cor 1:1; 9:1-2; 15:9; Gal 1:1,15-16; Eph 1:1; Col 1:1; 1 Tim 1:1; 2 Tim 1:1,11; Tit 1:1,3). Conversion and vision (Acts 9:3-22; 22:4-19; 26:9-15; 1 Cor 9:1; 15:8; Gal 1:13). Sent to Gentiles (Acts 13:2-3,47-48; 22:17-21; Rom 11:13; 15:16; Gal 1:15-24).

### 4. GOSPEL (score: 0.64)
Multiple designations: Gospel of the Kingdom (Mat 4:23), Gospel of God (Rom 1:1), Gospel of Christ (Rom 1:16; Gal 1:7), Preaching of Jesus Christ (Rom 16:25), Mystery of the Gospel (Eph 6:19), Word of Reconciliation (2 Cor 5:19).

### 5. REVELATION (score: 0.58)
Disclosure, manifestation. Paul received gospel by revelation (Gal 1:12), went to Jerusalem by revelation (Gal 2:2), received visions and revelations (2 Cor 12:1-4).

### 6. PREACHING (score: 0.56)
The act of exhorting, prophesying, reproving, teaching. Paul commissioned to preach to Gentiles.
