# Greek Parsing Raw Data

## 1 Thessalonians 4:15
Greek: Touto gar hymin legomen en logo Kyriou, hoti hemeis hoi zontes hoi perileipomenoi eis ten parousian tou Kyriou ou me phthasomen tous koimethentas

Key words:
- legomen: lego (G3004), Pres Act Ind 1P -- "we say/declare"
- hemeis: ego (G1473), Nom Pl -- "we"
- zontes: zao (G2198), Pres Act Ptcp Nom Pl M -- "the living ones"
- perileipomenoi: perileipomoi (G4035), Pres Pass Ptcp Nom Pl M -- "the remaining ones"
- parousian: parousia (G3952), Acc Sg F -- "coming/presence"
- phthasomen: phthano (G5348), Aor Act Subj 1P -- "we shall [not] precede" (ou me + aorist subjunctive = emphatic negation)
- koimethentas: koimaomai (G2837), Aor Pass Ptcp Acc Pl M -- "those having fallen asleep"

## 1 Corinthians 15:51
Greek: idou mysterion hymin lego; pantes ou koimethesometha, pantes de allagesometha

Key words:
- koimethesometha: koimaomai (G2837), Fut Pass Ind 1P -- "we shall sleep"
- allagesometha: allasso (G236), Fut Pass Ind 1P -- "we shall be changed"
- pantes: pas (G3956), Nom Pl M -- "all"

Note: "pantes ou koimethesometha" = "all shall not sleep" = "not all shall sleep"
"pantes de allagesometha" = "but all shall be changed"

## 2 Thessalonians 2:2
Greek: eis to me tacheos saleutheinai hymas... hos hoti enesteken he hemera tou Kyriou

Key words:
- enesteken: enistemi (G1764), Perf Act Ind 3S -- "has arrived / is present" (NOT "is at hand" in sense of future)
- hemera: hemera (G2250), Nom Sg F -- "day"
- The perfect tense indicates the Thessalonians believed the day had ALREADY come/was NOW present
