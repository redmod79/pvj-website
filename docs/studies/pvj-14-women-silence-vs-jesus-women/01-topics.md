# Topics — pvj-14: Women "Keep Silence" vs Jesus Teaching Women

- Religious privileges of among early Christians: ACT 1:14; 12:12,13; 1CO 11:5; 14:34; 1TI 2:11
- As prophets: Miriam (EXO 15:20,21; MIC 6:4), Deborah (JDG 4:4,5), Huldah (2KI 22:14-20), Anna (LUK 2:36-38), Philip's four daughters (ACT 21:9)
- Good instances include Priscilla (ACT 18:26), Phoebe (ROM 16:1,2), Lois and Eunice (2TI 1:5)
- Unclassified scriptures: 1CO 11:3-15; 14:34,35; 1TI 2:9-15; TIT 2:3-5
