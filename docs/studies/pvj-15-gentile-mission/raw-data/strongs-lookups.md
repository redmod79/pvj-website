# Raw Strong's Lookups

## G1484 — ethnos
158 occurrences, 15 translations. "Gentiles" 62x, "nations" 35x, "nation" 19x, "the Gentiles" 18x.
Definition: a race, tribe; specially a foreign (non-Jewish) one; by implication pagan.
Key: Same word used in Matt 28:19 ("all nations") and Rom 11:13 ("the Gentiles").

## G652 — apostolos
79 occurrences, 11 translations. "apostles" 49x, "an apostle" 16x.
Definition: a delegate; an ambassador of the Gospel; a commissioner of Christ.
Usage refs include: Acts 26:12-20 (Paul's commission account), Rom 16:7, 1 Cor 1:1, Gal 1:1.

## G4263 — probaton
20 occurrences, 4 translations. "sheep" 16x, "a sheep" 2x.
Definition: something that walks forward; a sheep.
Key verses: Matt 10:6; 15:24 ("lost sheep"); John 10:1-16 (Good Shepherd discourse); John 10:16 ("other sheep").

## G1672 — Hellen (Greek)
Found via semantic search. "Greeks" 6x, "a Greek" 3x.
Used in Paul's "Jew and Greek" formula: Rom 1:16; 10:12; Gal 3:28; Col 3:11.

## H1471 — gowy (nation, Gentile)
"nations" 97x, "the nations" 78x, "the heathen" 54x, "nation" 50x.
OT equivalent of G1484 ethnos. Used in Gen 12:3; 22:18; Ps 2:8; Isa 42:1; etc.
